About Me


Hi! I'm Khurram Ghullamani. I graduated in May 2017 with a degree in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My interests lie in full-stack web and mobile application development.

I am currently a Software Engineer at JPMorgan Chase in Chicago, working on Commercial Card's Technology Operation and Services platform. Outside of work, I enjoy biking on Chicago's trails and reading. My favorite books include Gone Girl, The Fault in our Stars and The Kite Runner.

I am very passionate about the intersection between Computer Science and education. In fact, I majored in Computer Science after my experience teaching a computer seminar to senior citizens and observing the power of technology to empower individuals. Two years ago, my roommate and I launched Code Camp, a seven week bootcamp aimed at teaching college students and young professionals programming fundamentals through web programming. Last summer, we had our second iteration of Code Camp, using Python. The third iteration of Code Camp is coming up during Summer 2018.

I've said all I wanted to say! Feel free to check out my resume as well as my projects on GitHub and connect with me on social networking sites. I am always open to new projects and opportunities!

Enjoy!
