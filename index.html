About Me


Hi! I'm Khurram Ghullamani. I graduated in May 2017 with a degree in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My interests lie in full-stack web and mobile application development and I have ample experience developing fullstack web applications and hybrid mobile applications through my internships at JPMorgan Chase, Truepad, and State Farm Research and Development Center.

During Summer 2016, I interned at JPMorgan Chase's Data Management team in Chicago and will be returning to the company as a full-time software developer starting July 2017. I was raised in Chicago and dearly love the city. Outside of work, I enjoy biking on Chicago's trails and reading. My favorite books to date include Gone Girl, The Fault in our Stars and The Kite Runner.

I am very passionate about the intersection between Computer Science and education. In fact, I majored in Computer Science after my experience teaching a computer seminar to senior citizens and observing the power of technology to empower individuals. Last summer, my roommate and I launched Code Camp, a seven week bootcamp aimed at teaching college students and young professionals programming fundamentals through web programming. This summer, summer 2017, we will launch the second iteration of Code Camp with Python.

I've said all I wanted to say! Feel free to check out my resume as well as my projects on GitHub and connect with me on social networking sites. I am always open to new projects and opportunities!
